About Me

Hi, I’m Yuma Uchiumi (@yumaloop), a graduate student majoring in computer science. I was born on December 8, 1997 in Tokyo, Japan. My research goal is to understand and implement the computation for human thinking; I study statistical algorithms, stochastic models, and information processing systems to solve the cognitive problems with human thinking.
